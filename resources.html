Company Event Feedback Survey Template

This company event feedback survey is designed to gather feedback and insights from your team to help improve future company events! This form is fully customizable to fit your needs.

Why is it a good idea to send an event follow-up survey?

Sending your team a post-event feedback survey not only gives employees a space where they can give anonymous feedback and suggestions, but it will also help you evaluate your event efforts and address any concerns directly. Plus, the feedback from the survey because a valuable resource when it comes to planning future company events!

How to use this company event feedback survey:

This template is available as a Google form or Word doc! It’s simple, streamlined, and totally customizable for your company’s needs. Simply download the resource and select the Make a copy button.
